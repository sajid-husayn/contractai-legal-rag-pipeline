EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

This Employment Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between ABC Corporation ("Company") and John Doe ("Employee").

TERMINATION PROCEDURES:
Either party may terminate this agreement with 30 days written notice. The Company may terminate the Employee immediately for cause, including but not limited to misconduct, breach of confidentiality, or failure to perform duties.

CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION:
Employee acknowledges that during employment, they may have access to confidential information including trade secrets, customer lists, and proprietary processes. Employee agrees to maintain strict confidentiality and not disclose such information to third parties.

PAYMENT TERMS:
Employee will receive a base salary of $75,000 per year, payable bi-weekly. Payment will be made via direct deposit within 2 business days of the end of each pay period.

INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY:
All work products, inventions, and intellectual property created by Employee during the course of employment shall be owned by the Company. Employee assigns all rights to such intellectual property to the Company.

BENEFITS:
Employee is entitled to health insurance, 15 days paid vacation annually, and participation in the company 401(k) plan with 3% company matching.

This agreement is governed by the laws of the State of California and shall be effective as of January 1, 2024.
